Our Story

A Legacy of Legal Excellence

Founded in 1991 by Christopher R. Bridge, our firm began with a simple yet powerful conviction: that every client deserves world-class legal representation delivered with personal care. What started as a boutique practice has grown — through decades of dedication, landmark victories, and an expanding team of exceptional attorneys — into one of the nation's most respected law firms.

Today, Christopher R. Bridge & Co. serves individuals, corporations, and institutions across the full spectrum of legal needs. Our attorneys have argued before state supreme courts and federal circuit courts, closed multi-billion dollar transactions, and guided clients through some of the most complex legal challenges of our era.

Yet through all our growth, one thing has never changed: our belief that exceptional legal counsel begins with listening — understanding each client's unique situation, goals, and concerns — and then delivering the strategic, relentless representation they deserve.

Our Journey

35 Years of Building Excellence

From a single office to a distinguished multi-practice firm, our journey reflects a steadfast commitment to growth, excellence, and the clients who have placed their trust in us.

Christopher R. Bridge establishes the firm in New York with a founding vision of client-centered excellence and strategic legal innovation.

1998

Expansion & Recognition
